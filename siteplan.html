WDD 231: Individual Website Project – Site Plan

Site Name

Explore Uganda

The site name was selected to represent a tourism-focused website dedicated to showcasing Uganda’s natural attractions, national parks, culture, and travel experiences. The name is short, memorable, and clearly communicates the purpose of the website.

Optional domain: exploreuganda.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide visitors with reliable information about tourist destinations in Uganda. The site will highlight popular places, travel tips, and featured attractions while allowing users to explore destinations dynamically using JavaScript-powered content.

Scenarios

  • What are the top tourist destinations to visit in Uganda?
  • How can I find details about national parks and attractions?

Color Scheme

The following colors will be used consistently across the website:

  • Forest Green (#1b5e20): Headings, navigation bar, footer
  • Light Sand (#f5f5dc): Page background
  • Dark Gray (#333333): Body text
  • Golden Accent (#c9a227): Buttons, links, highlights

Typography

  • Montserrat: Headings and navigation
  • Open Sans: Body text and paragraphs
