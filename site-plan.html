Site Name

EverSip

This name was selected because the website focuses on promoting healthy, refreshing beverages that can be enjoyed anytime, emphasizing an everlasting sip experience.

Optional domain availability: eversip.com

Site Purpose

The website provides information about different beverage products, recipes, and health benefits, while also offering an online store for customers to purchase drinks directly. It serves as a hub for beverage enthusiasts looking for both products and guidance on healthy drinking habits.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find detailed information about the health benefits of a specific beverage?
  • How can I purchase EverSip products online and get them delivered?

Color Scheme

  • Primary Color: #2a9d8f – used for headings, buttons, and accents.
  • Secondary Color: #264653 – used for body text and footer.
  • Background Color: #f4f4f4 – used for page background.

Typography

  • Montserrat: Used for all headings.
  • Roboto: Used for body text.
